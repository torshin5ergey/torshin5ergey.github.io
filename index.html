Hello! My name is Sergey Torshin, a creative coder based in Moscow. This is my digital generative computer graphics exhibition.

I'm exploring various techniques and tools for creating generative art. One of my favorite tools is p5.js, a JavaScript library that makes it incredibly easy to create and share visual art.

Below, you'll find a collection of my works, each one a unique exploration of algorithmic art. Basically there are cellular automata and rule-based systems. These sketches showcases the beauty and complexity that can arise from simple rules and random processes. Click on any image to delve deeper into the individual projects.
